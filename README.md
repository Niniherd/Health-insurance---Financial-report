# Health-insurance---Financial-report
Financial analysis of a group of SEDES within an insurance administration company.
#REQUEST
1. Power BI Visualization (Executive Dashboard)

I need an interactive tool that allows me to explore data across the 16 active branches. The dashboard should include the following components:

Liquidity Traffic Light View:
A visual comparing Previous Cash Balance vs Current Cash Balance by branch and month. I need to quickly identify which branches are ending the month in negative cash positions (as seen in some records from September 2025).

Operating Cycle Analysis:
A stacked bar chart showing key operational metrics: Client Collections vs Supplier Payments vs Costs and Expenses Payable. The goal is to evaluate whether we are collecting faster than we are paying.

Activity Breakdown:
A donut chart segmenting cash flow by Type of Activity: Operating, Investing, and Financing. This will help determine whether cash is being generated from operations or reliant on external financing.

Dynamic Filters:
Essential filters should include:

Year (2023, 2024, 2025)

Branch (SEDE1 to SEDE16)

Financial Closing Status (TRUE/FALSE)

📑 2. Written Report (Analysis & Insights)

I am not looking for a repetition of Excel figures. As an analyst, I expect you to explain the “why” behind the numbers:

Accounts Receivable Quality:
I’ve noticed changes in the Allowance for Doubtful Accounts. I need an assessment of whether increases in this metric in certain branches pose a risk to future liquidity.

Debt Structure Analysis:
Explain the evolution of financial obligations, both short-term and long-term (including IFRS 16 liabilities). Are we actually reducing debt, or simply refinancing it?

Branch Performance Ranking:
Rank branches based on Net Income and Net Cash Flow generation. I want to clearly identify top-performing clinics versus those requiring intervention.

Impact of Equity Revaluation:
Explain how asset valuations and equity revaluation are impacting the balance sheet, especially in projected data for October 2025.
